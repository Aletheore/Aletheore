# Constitutional Role Matrix

This matrix is informative and derived from the Draft Constitution. If this matrix conflicts with VDP-0000, VDP-0000 remains authoritative.

| Role | Phase | Core powers | Prohibitions | Appointment | Removal | Accountable to |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Constitutional Steward | Founder Stewardship | Maintain canonical repository; appoint initial Maintainers and Editors; authorize ordinary VDP transitions after gates pass; initiate emergency protective action; propose amendments; initiate institutional transition. | Must not bypass gates, fabricate review, claim AI approval, erase records, grant permanent extra-constitutional authority, accept amendments outside process, or continue unilateral authority after Council activation. | Initially Arihant Kaul; successor by public succession record before Council activation. | Ends when Constitutional Governance activates; interim replacement possible after 90 days unavailability. | Constitution and inspectable governance records. |
| Maintainer | Founder Stewardship or later phases | Review proposals and implementation; merge authorized changes; maintain assigned areas; participate where eligible; nominate contributors. | No automatic constitutional amendment authority, unilateral acceptance authority, unrestricted repository authority, or authority outside assigned scope. | Appointment record by eligible authority for the active phase. | Resignation, inactivity, suspension, or removal record. | Constitution, assigned area records, and active governance authority. |
| VDP Editor | Any phase | Verify metadata and format; assign or confirm identifiers; check lifecycle gates; coordinate reviews; record authorized transitions; identify process defects. | Must not approve own proposal by editorship, decide technical merit solely as editor, change normative meaning without authorization, or fabricate approval. | Appointment record by eligible authority. | Removal or inactivity record by eligible authority. | Constitution, VDP--001, and active governance authority. |
| Reviewer | Any phase | Provide scoped evaluation of architecture, security, performance, compatibility, governance, evidence, feasibility, or editorial correctness. | Listing as reviewer does not automatically imply approval; must not hide conflicts. | Review participation or assignment record. | Review scope may end by completion, withdrawal, conflict, or governance record. | Review record and active governance authority. |
| Contributor | Any phase | Author VDPs; submit implementations; report defects; challenge decisions; request review; fork under the license. | No governance authority merely by contribution; no guarantee of merge, appointment, acceptance, confidential access, or immunity from proportionate moderation. | Human participation in project work. | Moderation or restriction only through proportionate process. | Constitution, project records, and applicable community rules. |
| Working Group Lead | Any phase where chartered | Coordinate chartered scope, deliverables, reporting, and decision process. | Working Group activity or popularity does not create constitutional authority. | Working Group charter. | Charter dissolution, expiry, replacement, or removal record. | Working Group charter and active governance authority. |
| Steering Council Member | Constitutional Governance | Exercise permanent governance authority beneath the Constitution; vote on ordinary and constitutional decisions; handle final ordinary appeals. | Must not override Constitution, ignore conflicts, treat repository ownership as authority, or count abstentions as affirmative votes. | Council activation and membership records under the Constitution. | Term expiry, resignation, removal, vacancy procedure, or inactivity record. | Accepted Constitution and public Council decision records. |
| Interim Constitutional Steward | Pre-Council continuity only | Preserve continuity and initiate governance transition after Steward unavailability. | Must not become unrestricted founder replacement or permanently expand authority. | Active Maintainers appoint after 90 days unavailability, with two-thirds approval and at least two affirmative votes. | Ends when successor, Steward, or institutional governance is validly established. | Constitution, appointment record, and active Maintainers. |
